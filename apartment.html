INTRODUCTION

In the fast-paced city life, we seldom have the time to live in the moment. But this is about to change. Being the pioneers of integrated township development, The Hiranandani Group has brought its expertise to Oragadam. Nestled within lush landscaped gardens, this township is complete with every amenity you desire. From essentials like a world-class hospital, a school with a large playground, hypermarkets and retail stores, to luxury amenities like a well-designed clubhouse, swimming pools, a gym, yoga areas and multiple sports facilities, Hiranandani Parks is designed to cater to all your lifestyle needs.
